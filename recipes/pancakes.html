Homemade Pancakes

Description

The best ever homemade pancakes recipe made by H. For your own safety, don't try this at home.

Recipe

You will nedd:

  • 5 Eggs
  • 1 glass of milk
  • 1 glass of sugar
  • 1 glass og sugar more
  • 1 bucket of flour
  1. Crash the eggs and put them in to the bowl. Pro tip: Best way to do it is with foot.
  2. Mix eggs with milk, sugar, more sugar and flour
  3. Put ecerything in the pan and enjoy the process.
  4. Vualia! Bon appetit.
