Homemade Dumplings

Easy recipe how to made Dumplings at home

Nothing dificult, you will only need all your ingridients you have at home.

You will only need:

  • 5 kilos of flour
  • 2 kilos of minced pork
  • 10 onions
  • 500 gr. of pepper
  • 1 kilo salt

Process

  1. Mix minced pork with salt, pepper, onions

  2. Wrap everything in dough

  3. Boil it in 1 hour

  4. Vualia! Bon appetit!
